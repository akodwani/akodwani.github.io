DJ Tutor

01 / 04

Learning by seeing the system.

I am a visual learner. When I wanted to learn DJing, I found that most beginner guidance assumes you already understand the language of a DJ deck. That felt like a problem other people would face too, so I built DJ Tutor as a visual learning system: the deck lives in the browser, the next control is made clear, and every action is confirmed as you learn.

02 / 04

How to use DJ Tutor.

  1. Choose a lesson. Open Practice Studio and select a control to learn.
  2. Follow the deck. Use the highlighted control on the on-screen DJ deck, or connect your DJ deck through Web MIDI.
  3. Keep moving. Each action is confirmed in the lesson, then the next step becomes available.

04 / 04

Learning the instrument through the instrument.

The project mirrors the controller in the browser, listens for live MIDI from physical hardware, and keeps a simulator mode available for practice without the deck connected. Each lesson focuses attention on one control, confirms the matching action, and moves the learner forward.
